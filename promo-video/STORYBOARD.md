# Parallel Memory Promo Storyboard

## Beat 1: Hook

- Time: 0.0-3.2s
- Layout: Large PM mark on the left, oversized title, floating playing cards on the right.
- Motion: Brand mark stamps in, title rises, cards fan and flip in sequence.
- Transition out: Push slide to the app surface.

## Beat 2: App Surface

- Time: 3.2-6.9s
- Layout: Real desktop screenshot in a framed browser surface, feature chips on the left.
- Motion: Screenshot eases in and slowly scales; chips enter from alternating directions; stat rail ticks on.
- Transition out: Push slide.

## Beat 3: Gameplay Loop

- Time: 6.9-10.6s
- Layout: Stylized 4x4 board with a right-side turn rail.
- Motion: Cards flip, matches lock, progress fills, active board indicator advances.
- Transition out: Push slide.

## Beat 4: Result and Share

- Time: 10.6-13.7s
- Layout: Result modal on the left, mobile screenshot in a phone frame on the right.
- Motion: Rank badge scales in, result stats count on, mobile screen slides into place.
- Transition out: Blur crossfade.

## Beat 5: CTA

- Time: 13.7-16.0s
- Layout: Center-left brand lockup, simple challenge copy, small card row.
- Motion: Elements enter with restrained confidence; final fade to black.
